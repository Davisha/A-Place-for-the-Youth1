A Place for the Youth

By Davisha Bonnie

About me

Well my name is Davisha Bonnie and I'm 17. I live in the island nation of Antigua and Barbuda and I recently attended the antigua Girls High School. I never really thought about engaging in my community much, it was just a thought in the back of my head. This course gave me the courage to try to make a difference on a topic that I know may countries among mine struggle with.

The Problem

Over the years I've noticed that the youth of my community are losing their way. More and more kids around my age have no where to hangout and just be kids. Many of them have turn to drug use and violence and I believe that together we can change that for a better future. I've decided that I can't sit back and watch anymore, It's time for us all to take action.

What's the plan?

The plan's to start an organization just for kids. I want to start all sorts of clubs for cooking, sports, art, music and many more. We'll first raise funds to build a community center just for this organization, then we can bring in professionals in different fields who can teach the kids. It dosn't have to end in Antigua and Barbuda, why should it when there's the entire Caribbean and lots more. This can go so far and reach so many more kids worldwide just like me. Just think about how far these kids could go. As for the organization itself lets call it A Place for the Youth!
